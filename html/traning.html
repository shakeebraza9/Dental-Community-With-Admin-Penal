Outstanding.

Being a registered manager can be challenging, but not knowing what your day to day responsibilities are can be daunting. 90% of registered providers do not feel confident in managing compliance as part of their role.

The one day compliance training course is designed to give you an in depth knowledge and understanding of managing compliance within your day to day job role. It highlights the responsibilities as a CQC registered managers, and gives you an understanding of the CQC KLOE and how health and social care services are inspected The course is delivered by our expert and experienced team of consultant.

The course is designed to cater all types of Health & Social Care services regulated by the CQC. If you are a registered manager, nominated individual or even a practice manager this course is the key foundation to understanding the regulations and legislation.

This course is for :

  • Registered Managers
  • Nominated Individuals
  • Service Managers
  • Business Owners
  • Practice Managers
  • Compliance Consultants
  • Governance and compliance Lead

What you will learn :

  • Understanding the role and responsibilities of a registered manager
  • Define ‘good governance’ and demonstrate understanding of good governance
  • Understanding what statuary notification are and when to report them
  • The CQC fundamental standards and how these have changed
  • How CQC monitor and inspect services
  • How to conduct a mock inspection
  • The key legislations and regulations you must know
  • Dealing with regulation breaches
  • How to create evidence matrix
  • New CQC Assessment framework
  • How to manage compliance stress-free
  • How to ensure your team are prepared for a CQC Inspection
  • 5 top tips to pass a CQC Inspection with flying colours
  • 3 years service with radiation protection advisor
